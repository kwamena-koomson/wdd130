Frequently Asked Questions?

White water rafting is an adventurous water activity involving navigating rivers in inflatable rafts through various levels of rough water, known as rapids. Yes, with proper safety measures, experienced guides, and appropriate equipment, white water rafting can be a thrilling yet safe adventure.

Generally, we recommend children aged 8 and above to fully enjoy the experience, but it ultimately depends on the specific trip. Contact us for age-related queries. Dress comfortably and be prepared to get wet. Wear swimwear or synthetic clothing that dries quickly. Sunscreen, water shoes, and a hat are recommended.

No prior experience is required for most trips. Our experienced guides will provide safety instructions and training before the trip. Yes, we offer trips for all skill levels. You can choose from mild, moderate, or challenging rapids, depending on your experience and comfort level.

We highly recommend waterproof cameras or phone cases. However, our guides can take photos of your trip, available for purchase. It's advisable not to bring valuable items on the trip to prevent potential loss or damage due to water exposure.
